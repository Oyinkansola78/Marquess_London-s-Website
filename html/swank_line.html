Swank Line

Our SWANK line is the ultimate Marquess experience, for the Marquess Man looking to build a long lasting, archivable wardrobe of staples conveying his timeless yet contemporary style and versatility. With our expansive custom selections, these pieces are designed to last.

1

CONSTRUCTION

From shoulder padding, sleeve head options, to horsehair body canvases and lining, the SWANK line provides extensive premium construction options, that guarantee your garments provide a distinguished satisfaction that improves over time. Our SWANK line garments blend hand sewing and machine-made methods, to provide quality superiority and a luxurious finish. Ideal for the today’s man.

2

FABRICS

With fabrics from world-renowned fabric purveyors that include Holland & Sherry, Loro Piana, E. Zegna, Drago, Reda, VBC, and more. SWANK line provides an extensive range of premium quality cloths, patterns, and weights. Suitable for all seasons and occasions, designed to accompany the Marquess Man for years to come. Widely ranging from wool, linen, cotton, silk and their blends.

3

FITTINGS

The SWANK Line fitting process uses both your body measurements and body posture assessment. This ensures a superior fit, personalised to your movements, and natural body positions, offering a magnificent look and feel with optimum comfort, unique to your build and lifestyle.
